NEG-OC-004

Title: Register new account without accepting privacy policy

Preconditions: must have a valid email address

Steps to Perform:

1: navigate to https://demo.opencart.com/

2: click the "My account" drop down in the top-right

3: choose "register" in the drop down menu

4: fill in the necessary credentials - First Name, Last Name, and so on

5: choose whether or not you want to subscribe by clicking the bottom-left switcher

6: click "continue"

7: receive an error: "warning: You Must Agree to the privacy policy!"

Expected Results: should be able to successfully create an account with proper credentials

Actual Results: cannot successfully create the account without agreeing to the privacy policy

Status: PASS

Notes: the "privacy policy" and "subscribe" switchers need to be more clearly highlighted to prevent this error from occurring.
