Title: unable to navigate to 'Desktops', 'Components' or 'Laptops & Notebooks' from main page

Severity: Medium

Priority: Low

Preconditions: must be registered and logged in

1.navigate to https://demo.opencart.com/en-gb?route=common/home 
2. click "My Account" in the top-right 
3. choose "login" from the drop-down

6. the buttons do not respond
7. click any of the drop-down options (IE:'Macs'); notice these respond
8. click any of the other navigation bar options, such as 'Tablets' or 'Software'; notice these respond as well
